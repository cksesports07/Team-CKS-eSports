Frequently Asked Questions
What game does CKS Esports compete in? ▾
CKS Esports competes exclusively in Rocket League. We are a dedicated Rocket League organization focused on building a competitive team capable of entering structured league and tournament play.
How do I try out for CKS Esports? ▾
Fill out the tryout application form in the Recruitment section above. Include your in-game name, rank, platform, Discord, and any gameplay clips you'd like us to review. We evaluate all applications and follow up via Discord or email.
Is CKS Esports looking for sponsors? ▾
Yes. We are actively seeking founding partners and sponsors. Email us at sponsors@cksesports.com with your inquiry or use the contact form to request our sponsorship package. Early partnerships carry unique long-term value.
When does Season 1 competition begin? ▾
We are currently in the formation phase of Season 1. Our roster is confirmed and we are evaluating tournament entries. Match dates will be announced on our website and social channels as they are confirmed. Follow us to stay updated.
Can I contribute to CKS as a content creator or designer? ▾
Absolutely. We are open to working with talented content creators, graphic designers, video editors, and social media managers who align with our brand vision. Reach out via email with examples of your work and your areas of interest.
Where can I watch CKS matches live? ▾
Live match coverage will be available through our Twitch channel and YouTube once competitive play begins. Player streams will also be available through their personal channels. Follow our social media for live alerts.
